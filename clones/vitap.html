WELCOME TO VIT-AP UNIVERSITY


About VIT-AP :

VIT-AP UNIVERSITY , AMARAVATHI is an private university in Andhra Pradesh with International facilities

The VIT group of institutions is well known for its academic excellence, outstanding infrastructure, innovations in education, unbeatable placements, training, industrial partnerships and international relations. VIT-AP located at Amaravati, the capital of Andhra Pradesh provides a holistic environment for overall personality development of its students. We inspire bright young minds from across the country and the world, to develop and harness their exceptional intellectual abilities to become socially responsible and skilled individuals. Based on a meticulously designed curriculum with industry and international academia feedback, the students are offered a range of diverse courses, and are provided the opportunity to chart their own course.

With a strong emphasis on recreation, the institution provides not just the students but the faculty and staff with facilities that keeps them active, involved, and healthy.
